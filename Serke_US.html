About the Game

Serke is a hack-and-slash action game where you fight against a virus invasion. As the AI purification program 'Serke,' you must purify enemies infected by a malicious virus and save the virtual world.

Use five unique weapons (Sword, Rifle, Halberd, Heavy Weapon, Gauntlet, etc.) to quickly defeat endless waves of enemies and engage in strategic action through a dynamic combat system. A futuristic sci-fi setting and overwhelming hack-and-slash action await you.

Weapons

Cyanea

Cyanea is a one-handed sword with excellent mobility.
It's a well-balanced weapon with decent stats in all areas.

Spinefire

Spinefire is a rifle that allows you to summon drones and turrets to fight on the move.
This weapon is ideal for attacking enemies from a distance.

Red Claw

Red Claw is a large, halberd-type melee weapon.
Crush your enemies in a single blow with its powerful damage.

Imperator

Imperator is a powerful long-range weapon that uses firepower.
Annihilate enemies with high-damage equipment like shotguns, gatling guns, laser cannons, and grenades.

Death Stalker

Death Stalker is a melee weapon for close-quarters combat.
Its kicks and punches allow for fast-paced, high-speed battles.
